**Output Location:** `danny-actors/reports/plan_2026-06-19_15-35-21.md`

---

## 1. Executive Summary & Goal
The objective is to design, build, and deploy an automated B2B outreach actor named **`PitchPilot — Investor Finder & Cold Pitch Writer`** (slug: `pitch-pilot-investor-outreach`) under the custom `danny-actors/` namespace. 

This Actor will enable the user to input a B2B SaaS application profile (starting with **"health-erecords"**) and automatically:
1. Sift through target investor websites (VC portals, angel lists, and search queries).
2. Scrape focus criteria, team members, contact options, and current portfolio listings.
3. Assess the investor's relevance (e.g. do they invest in HealthTech, B2B SaaS, or digital health?).
4. Generate a highly customized cold email copy and investment proposal tailored directly to that investor's historical portfolio and investment thesis.

---

## 2. Scraping Strategy (Bypassing Anti-Bot Measures)
Because major venture platforms like Crunchbase and PitchBook employ aggressive Cloudflare anti-bot firewalls, the actor will rely on a robust two-layer sourcing architecture:

* **Layer 1: Google/DuckDuckGo Dorks (Free discovery)**:
  - If no URLs are provided, the actor will search public indices for search patterns (e.g., `site:linkedin.com/in/ "Healthcare VC"`, `"HealthTech seed fund" portfolio`).
  - Scrapes the search engine results to compile a list of target VC domains.

* **Layer 2: Direct VC Domain Crawling**:
  - The actor uses Crawlee's `CheerioCrawler` to crawl the team pages, portfolio directories, and contact forms of those target VC websites.
  - Extracts phone numbers, contact emails, and social handles (LinkedIn/Twitter) directly from the sites.
  - Summarizes the firm's thesis (e.g., "Seed-stage enterprise healthcare software").

---

## 3. The App Profile: "health-erecords"
For the initial run, the app profile will be pre-configured for the **health-erecords** product:
- **Product Name**: `health-erecords`
- **Niche/Industry**: HealthTech / B2B SaaS / HIPAA-compliant Electronic Health Records
- **Core Value Prop**: Sleek, modern EHR platform that automates patient intake, streamlines charting for private practices, integrates billing, and connects to labs via HL7 APIs, saving doctors 10+ hours of admin work per week.
- **Investment Hook**: HealthTech VCs want to fund enterprise efficiency and AI integration in clinical workflows. We will pitch how `health-erecords` solves doctor burnout.

---

## 4. Personalized Cold Outreach Drafting
The actor will compile the scraped VC focus tags, existing portfolio companies, and target partner names. It will then pass this data to a prompt constructor:

* **With OpenAI Key**: The actor calls the OpenAI Chat Completion API (`gpt-4o-mini` or similar) to write a unique, warm outreach letter referencing their portfolio.
* **Without API Key (Fallback)**: A rule-based semantic template engine will stitch together the personalized pitch:
  > *"Dear [Partner Name], I saw that [VC Name] recently backed [Portfolio Company Name] in the digital health space. We're launching **health-erecords**, a HIPAA-compliant B2B SaaS that automates clinical charting for medical practices..."*
